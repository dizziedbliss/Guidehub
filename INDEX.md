A grouping of related branches:
- CS Stream = CS, CI, CB branches
- EE Stream = EE, EC, VL branches
- ME Stream = ME, RB branches
- CV Stream = CV branch

â†’ See: [ARCHITECTURE.md](./ARCHITECTURE.md)

### **What is "In Team" checking?**
Prevents students from joining multiple teams by checking the `team_members` table.

â†’ See: [SETUP_GUIDE.md](./SETUP_GUIDE.md)

Sequential counter using KV store: MCE26IP001, MCE26IP002, etc.
